# Scoring Framework

Score each opportunity from 1 to 5 across these dimensions:

| Dimension | Question |
| --- | --- |
| Mission | Is the problem meaningful and motivating? |
| Role fit | Does the role use and stretch core strengths? |
| Leadership | Are the leaders credible, capable, and aligned? |
| Market | Is there durable market potential? |
| Growth | Does the role expand future optionality? |
| Economics | Are compensation and risk appropriately balanced? |
| Timing | Is this the right moment for both sides? |

Weight dimensions when context warrants it, document the weights, and record confidence alongside every score.
